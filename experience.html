About Me

My name is Sylvia Necula and I'm a second year at Georgia Tech double majoring in Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering. My biomedical engineering background allows me to have a strong knowledge of physiology and problem solving, while my computer science background gives me the tools necessary to be an efficient programmer. I am excited about software development in the context of the medical field.

In my spare time, I like to sail, scuba dive, travel, and cook.
